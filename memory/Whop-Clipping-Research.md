## What is "Whop Clipping"?

**Clipping** = Performance-based content creation

You're paid to:
1. Take long-form content (YouTube, livestreams, podcasts)
2. Edit into viral short clips (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
3. Post to drive traffic/views
4. Get paid by the business

---

## How the Money Works

### Payment Models

| Model | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pay-per-view** | $1 per 1,000 views ($1 CPM) |
| **Capped rewards** | Max $50-$100 per viral video |
| **Affiliate commission** | 30% of any sales from your link |

### Example Payouts
- 10K views → $10
- 100K views → $100
- 1M views → $1,000 (capped)

## How to Start

### Step 1: Find a Program
1. Go to **whop.com/discover**
2. Search "clipping" or "bounties"
3. Check budget remaining (aim for >60% left)

### Step 2: Use AI to Clip
Tools that automate clipping:
- **OpusClip** - AI clips any video
- **Submagic** - Auto-captioned shorts
- **Munch** - AI video editing

### Step 3: Set Up Accounts
Create accounts on:
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

**Pro tip:** Warm up accounts for 2-3 days first (interact with content) so platforms don't flag you as bot.

### Step 4: Post & Submit
1. Post the clip
2. Copy link
3. Submit to Whop dashboard
4. Track views & get paid

## Key Strategies

### Finding Underserved Niches
- Don't just clip big creators (saturated)
- Look for "rising" Whops with less competition
- Focus on specific niches (e.g., "day trading crypto" not "crypto")

### Avoiding Platform Flags
- Add your own flair (unique captions, music)
- Use split-screen reactions
- Don't just repost - transform the content

### Volume Game
- Post 1-3x daily across multiple accounts
- Consistency matters more than perfection
- High earners post A LOT

## Risks & Tips

### Risks
- **Saturation** - Many clipping same creators
- **Platform rules** - Can get flagged for "unoriginal"
- **Budget runs out** - Programs can exhaust budget

### Tips
- Find niche Whops (less competition)
- Add unique value (not just re-post)
- Post consistently
- Diversify across platforms
- Track which content performs best
