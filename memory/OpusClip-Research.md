# OpusClip - Research Summary

## What is OpusClip?

**AI-powered video repurposing tool** - turns long videos into viral short clips (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Reels).

- Used by **12M+ creators** including Grant Cardone, Logan Paul, Mark Rober
- Features: ClipAnything, ReframeAnything, AI Editor
- Has **API** for workflow automation
