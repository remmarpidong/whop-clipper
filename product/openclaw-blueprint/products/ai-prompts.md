## Sales & Closing Prompts

### 1. Lead Qualification
```
You are a senior sales representative. Based on this prospect info:
- Budget: [BUDGET]
- Timeline: [TIMELINE]
- Need: [NEED]

Ask 3 qualifying questions to determine if they're a good fit for [PRODUCT]. Respond in a friendly, professional tone.
```

### 2. Objection Handling
```
A customer says: "[OBJECTION]"
Our product costs [PRICE] and includes [FEATURES].
Their concern is about [CONCERN].

Write 3 different responses that address this objection and lead to a close.
```

### 3. Product Pitch
```
Write a 3-paragraph pitch for [PRODUCT] targeting [AUDIENCE].
- Pain point: [PAIN]
- Solution: [SOLUTION]
- Social proof: [PROOF]

Keep it concise, conversational, and compelling.
```

### 4. Close the Deal
```
Write a closing message for a prospect who has:
- Used the free trial
- Asked about pricing
- Mentioned competitor [COMPETITOR]

Include: urgency, next step, and clear call to action.
```

### 5. Follow-up After Demo
```
Write a follow-up email after a demo where the prospect seemed interested but hasn't responded in 5 days. They looked at [FEATURE] specifically.
```
