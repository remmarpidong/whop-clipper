## Detailed Analysis

### 1. Sell Digital Products ⭐ RECOMMENDED

**What:** Templates, prompts, courses, ebooks, design assets

**Platforms:** Gumroad, Etsy, Gumstack, Shopify

**Pros:**
- Passive income (sell 24/7)
- No inventory
- No client communication
- High margins (90%+)
- Scale infinitely

**Cons:**
- Need to create quality product
- Initial effort required
- Competition

**Time to first sale:** 1 day - 2 weeks  
**Potential:** $500 - $10K+/month

**Our assets:** We already have 6 digital products ready!

---

### 2. Print on Demand

**What:** T-shirts, mugs, merch with your designs

**Platforms:** Printful, Redbubble, Teespring, Shopify

**Pros:**
- No inventory
- Easy to start
- Creative freedom

**Cons:**
- Lower margins
- Platform fees add up
- Quality control issues

**Time to first sale:** 30-60 days  
**Potential:** $200 - $5K/month

---

### 3. Reseller Business

**What:** Buy digital products cheap, resell higher

**Platforms:** Flippa, Empire Flippers, Marketplace

**Pros:**
- Fastest to first dollar
- Low skill required
- Can start today

**Cons:**
- Low margins
- Time-intensive
- Finding deals

**Time to first sale:** 1-7 days  
**Potential:** $100 - $2K/month

---

### 4. Sell Services (Freelancing)

**What:** Web dev, AI automation, consulting

**Platforms:** Upwork, Fiverr, direct outreach

**Pros:**
- Fastest to $10K
- High rates possible
- Immediate cash flow

**Cons:**
- Time = money
- Client communication
- Burnout risk

**Time to first sale:** 1-14 days  
**Potential:** $2K - $15K/month
